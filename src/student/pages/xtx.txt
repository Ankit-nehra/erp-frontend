Important Database Change

student_profiles DOES NOT store

session_id

class_id

section_id

Instead it stores

session

class_name

section

which are TEXT values.

Therefore students are fetched using JOIN.

Example:

student_profiles.class_name

=

classes.class_name

student_profiles.section

=

sections.section_name
